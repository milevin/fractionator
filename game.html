Objective: Solve the flying fraction equations as accurately as you can! Enter your answer in the answer box.

How to Play:

  • Watch for the flying fraction equation.
  • Type the numerator (top number) of your answer.
  • Hit SPACE to move to the denominator (bottom number).
  • Only 2 digits are allowed for the numerator. The input will automatically switch to the denominator after entering 2 digits.
  • Use the BACKSPACE key to correct mistakes. If you backspace the denominator completely, the input will switch back to the numerator.
  • Submit your answer by entering two digits in the denominator or hitting SPACE after one digit. A happy or sad face will tell whether your answer is correct.
